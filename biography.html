Biography

Name: James Gosling

Years: May 19, 1955 - Present

Life: James Gosling, born in Calgary Alberta, Canada, spent his early years fascinated by technology and computers. Growing up with a passion for programming, his early experiences laid the foundation for his later groundbreaking contributions to the world of computer science.

Significance in Computer Science: James Gosling is known as the "Father of Java," creating one of the most widely used programming languages.
