Biography

Name: James Gosling

Years: May 19, 1955 - Present

Education: William Aberhart High School, Carnegie Mellon University (1983), University of Calgary (1977)

Life: James Gosling, born in Calgary Alberta, Canada, spent his early years fascinated by technology and computers. Growing up with a passion for programming, his early experiences laid the foundation for his later groundbreaking contributions to the world of computer science. Gosling has two children, Katie and Kelsey, who are half siblings from Gosling's two marriages.

Significance in Computer Science: James Gosling is known as the "Father of Java," creating one of the most widely used programming languages, java. He also built satellite data acquisition systems, a multiprocessor version of Unix, multiple compliers, mail systems, and window managers. (School of information science)
